Strawberry Shortcake

⏱️ 45 mins • 🍽️ Serves 4 • 🌟 Easy

🧂 Ingredients Needed

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • Fresh strawberries
  • Whipped cream

👩‍🍳 Cooking Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  3. Add butter and milk, mix to form dough.
  4. Drop spoonfuls onto baking tray and bake for 15–20 mins.
  5. Slice shortcakes, layer with strawberries and cream.
  6. Serve with a smile and a sprinkle of love! 💗

💡 Tips & Notes

Let the shortcakes cool slightly before adding cream. You can replace strawberries with any berries you love!
